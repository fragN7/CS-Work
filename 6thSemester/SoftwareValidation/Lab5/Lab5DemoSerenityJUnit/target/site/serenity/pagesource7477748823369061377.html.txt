<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=triple_double&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Noun"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><span class="headword-line"><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/triple#English" title="triple">triple</a> <a href="/wiki/double#English" title="double">double</a></strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/triple_doubles#English" title="triple doubles">triple doubles</a></b>)</span>
</p>
<ol><li><span class="usage-label-sense"><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/basketball#English" title="basketball">basketball</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span></span> The achievement of a two-digit number of any three of <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/point#English" title="point">points</a> scored, <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/assist#English" title="assist">assists</a>, <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/rebound#English" title="rebound">rebounds</a>, <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/block#English" title="block">blocks</a>, or <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/steal#English" title="steal">steals</a> in a single <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/game#English" title="game">game</a>.
